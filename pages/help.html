Help & Support

Find answers to frequently asked questions and get help from our support team.

Getting Started

Click Register and choose to sign up with your phone number or email address. You will receive a 6-digit OTP to verify your account. After verification, you are automatically logged in.
Go to the Login page and enter the email address or phone number you registered with, plus your password. Make sure your account is verified before logging in.
After logging in you will be taken to the Plans page. Choose a subscription plan that fits your goals. MealMatrix will then help you set up your profile and generate personalized meal plans.

Using MealMatrix

After setting your goals, MealMatrix automatically generates a personalized weekly meal plan with recipes matched to your calorie and macro targets.
Yes! You can swap recipes, adjust portion sizes, and regenerate plans as many times as you want until you find the perfect fit for your lifestyle.
Log your meals daily through the dashboard. MealMatrix tracks your nutrition intake and shows you progress toward your daily calorie and macro goals.

Account & Billing
